                                                        GUIDELINES AND CRITERIA

                                            PLEASE READ GUIDELINE AND CRITERIA ACCORDINGLY

 

                                                  DO NOT HESITATE TO CONTACT OUR COMMITTEE FOR ASSISTANCE

 

  • Language: English

  • Duration: 3 minutes (MAX)

  • Please insert BIME logo at the top left in the video

  • File size: Not more than 100Mb

  • Small poster size: 300 x 300

  • Include a small white box on the right top of your poster dimension (120 x 60)

  • Do not put BIME logo on the small poster

  • The title of the project should be clear and readable

  • Large Poster size: A4 Size

  • Must insert UiTM Cawangan Melaka and BIME logo at the top left of the poster

  • 2 pages (MAX)

  • Must include:

    • - Project Introduction

    - Objective of the project

    - Target Audience (user)

    - Problem Statement

    - Novelty

    - Commercialization Idea

    - Project Walk-through (Demo)
