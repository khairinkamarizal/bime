WHAT IS BIME?

TIME TO UNLEASH YOUR CREATIVITY

BIME 2022 is a platform for UiTM student to gain new experience by exploring their creativity side and learning how to market their project by developing the prototype that they once visualized.

PAST WINNERS:

TEAL EDUCATION

The Teal Application is an educational phone app that focuses on learning art for students. The application provides lessons such as color theory, type of shapes and type of forms. Other than that, this application has a few quizzes and games. Our dream is to improve the students knowledge and make it easier for to understand art subjects!

See the VIDEO 
MONOTOME

Students are able to learn from everywhere through various platforms. Hence, Monotome had the initiative to make an interactive slide based on the theme given which is education. Our slides provide the education of English subjects. We decided to choose “Grammar” as the main concern of our application prototype targeted for Year 6 students.

See the video
THE SOLAR SYSTEM

The Solar System have created a free downloadable interactive web that are accessible online and offline. Our application interface consists of factual and concise information that are easy to understand and in relation with the subject Science. Our interactive application educational level is suitable for children and teenagers.
